Realize
your potential.

The Quantum rehabilitation department is built on expertise. Our top-notch rehab specialists have experience in a wide range of fields and are equipped to provide therapy for a range of conditions, including:

  • • Post-orthopedic surgery
  • • Joint replacement
  • • Post-stroke
  • • Post-amputation
  • • Pain management
  • • Speech, language, and swallowing disorders

Realize your potential in our spacious gymnasium, newly constructed to house all of the latest rehabilitation equipment. You will work with your therapist up to 7 days a week to set goals, face challenges, and grow stronger.

Strap yourself in for truly cutting-edge therapy with our newly acquired SMART Balance Master, a powerful tool in improving balance and coordination. This interactive, video game-like experience monitors a user’s balance control with dynamic force plates, presenting on-screen feedback to our therapists in real time.

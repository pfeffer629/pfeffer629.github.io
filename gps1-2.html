A brief introduction to pair programming by Mark and Devon

What is pair programming?

n. 1. Working on a programming project with a partner.

Why is pair programming important to DBC?

  1. It helps us learn from and teach each other.
  2. Helps solve problems better by using a collective mind.
  3. Helps us learn to be better colleagues.
  4. Teaches teamwork
  5. Makes us more comfortable with our classmates.
  6. Gives us practice working with others after DBC.

Driver

The person with his hands on the wheel(keyboard) while coding.

Navigator

The person with the plan, who tells the driver where to go.

Reflection

This GPS was very helpful. It solidified some of the concepts that I was struggling with in HTML and CSS. It was motivating to have a partner who knew the material so well. We had to find information online on how to do some of the things, but ended up finding a solution to all of our problems

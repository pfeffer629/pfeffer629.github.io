Nursing at Quantum is built upon a singular, simple ethos, one that gives our program a unique, personal touch: compassion and care above all else. Our staff tends to your every need, ensuring not only that you receive proper clinical care when necessary, but that you are made to feel welcome, involved, and important to the community. Quantum nurses go the extra mile to develop personal bonds with our residents, and we encourage you to let us know of any ways that we can make your stay with us more comfortable.

“My staff and I make every effort to befriend our residents. We believe that empathy is at the core of a successful nursing program.”
Tanya Randolph
Director of Nursing Services

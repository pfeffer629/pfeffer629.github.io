ABOUT EMC

Every Mother Counts, a non-profit organization dedicated to making pregnancy and childbirth safe for ever mother, has introduced the Orange Rose as a symbol of what we can accomplish together. Its everlasting bloom represents a cycle that ends in life, not death. For more information visit: http://www.everymothercounts.org

THE ORANGE ROSE CAMPAIGN

This Mother’s Day, Every Mother Counts will introduce the "orange rose" as a recognizable symbol of both our mission and a united effort to make pregnancy and childbirth safer for every mother, every where. The rose is both fragile and resilient. Under the right conditions, a flower will bloom. When a mother gives birth, so too begins the process of blooming into motherhood. However, without proper care and support in this phase she cannot thrive.

Each year, over 300,000 women will lose their lives to pregnancy and childbirth related causes. That’s 800 women lost, this Mother’s Day, from largely preventable causes. We know how to end these deaths, but we can’t do it alone. That’s why we’re hoping this campaign will inspire a movement to help us get women and mothers the care they need to survive.
